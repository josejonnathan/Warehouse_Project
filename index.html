OWS

OWS (Origin Warehouse Solutions) is a complette solution designed to efficiently manage the flow of products and goods within a storage environment, whether in a company, distribution center, or any other type of storage facility. This type of software is used to optimize inventory management, enhance visibility of stored products, and streamline logistics operations.

Tracking
Batch and Serial Number Tracking: Ideal for companies that handle products with batches or serial numbers, enabling precise tracing of product traceability. OWS Helps assign specific locations to each product in the warehouse, improving efficiency in product search and storage.
Stock and inventory
OWS allows for detailed tracking of stored products, including information such as quantity, location, date of receipt, and expiration date if applicable. Movement Tracking: Records all product entries and exits, making it easy to track operations and identify potential losses or discrepancies.
Cost Control
OWS allows you to allocate costs associated with the warehouse operations to specific products, customers, or projects. It helps in understanding how costs are distributed across various aspects of the business and enables detailed cost analysis. It can also assist in budgeting and financial planning for the warehouse and supply chain management.
Reporting
OWS provides the capability to generate reports and analyses that assist in making informed decisions regarding inventory management and storage operations, other systems can be integrated with enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems, point of sale (POS) systems, and other related systems for a more holistic supply chain management.
